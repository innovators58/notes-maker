PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy and Security page of the Notes Maker and Reminder App. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide you with the best experience. This includes your account information, notes, and reminders. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.

Data Storage

All your data is securely stored on our servers with encryption to protect it from unauthorized access. We ensure regular backups and apply the best security practices.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely for the purpose of improving your experience with the app. We use aggregated data to enhance our services and never sell your data to third parties.

Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption and secure authentication processes, to safeguard your information.

User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, and delete your data at any time. If you have any concerns or requests regarding your data, please contact our support team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy and data security, feel free to contact us at [support@notesapp.com].
